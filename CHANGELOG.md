## [2.2.0] - 2025-12-20
- Added enhancements to the search dialog and search UX.

## [2.1.0] - 2025-12-13
- Refinements to TOC numbering and dropdown behaviors.
- Stability fixes for the site build and content sync.
- Small performance improvements across core components.

## [2.0.0] - 2025-12-06
- Major refactor and stability release.
- Introduced modular CLI tooling for content lifecycle management.
- Reworked theme internals for better extensibility and maintainability.
